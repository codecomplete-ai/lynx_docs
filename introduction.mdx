## Create an Account

1. Visit [uselynx.ai](https://uselynx.ai) and click sign in.
2. Fill in the "Join Waitlist" form and reach out to your Lynx representative to approve your account.
3. Once your account is approved, you will receive an email with instructions to create your account.
4. Create an account with your credentials and click "Register as an Individual" or, if you've been separately instructed to do so, click "Join Organization".
5. Set up integrations and instructions.
